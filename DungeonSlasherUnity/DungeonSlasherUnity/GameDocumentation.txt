Dungeon Slasher Documentation. 
Game Control: The player can move by pressing the ADSW Keys on their keyboard. With left click he can attack.
Game Purpose. The player finds himself in a dungeon and wants to find the exit. The aim of the player is to find the exit.
Defeat, win conditions: The player loses when the goblins kill him and the player wins when he finds the exit of the dungeon.
Unity Version:2020.3.8f1.
